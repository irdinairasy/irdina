üçö Nasi Lemak

Click here to watch recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Coconut milk
  • Pandan leaves, ginger, lemongrass, and salt
  • Dried chili, shallots, garlic, ginger, belacan
  • Peanuts & ikan bilis (anchovies)
  • Tamarind water, sugar, and salt
  • Boiled egg
  • Cucumber slices

How to Cook

  1. Rice:
    1. Wash the rice until clean. Put it in a rice cooker or pot.
    2. Add coconut milk, water, pandan leaves, ginger, lemongrass, and salt. Stir well.
    3. Cook until the rice is done and water is absorbed. Fluff with a fork.
  2. Ikan Bilis & Peanuts:
    1. Heat oil in a wok. Fry the ikan bilis until crispy, remove and drain.
    2. Fry the peanuts until golden, remove and drain.
  3. Sambal:
    1. Grind dried chili (remove seeds), shallots, garlic, ginger, and belacan with a little oil into a paste.
    2. Stir-fry the paste until fragrant and oil separates.
    3. Add tamarind water, sugar, and salt. Cook over low heat until thick and dark red.
  4. Serving:
    1. Place rice on a plate.
    2. Garnish with sambal, fried anchovies, peanuts, cucumber slices, and boiled egg.
    3. Optional: Serve with spicy fried chicken or rendang.
